Privacy Policy for Arrow Game

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Arrow Game ("the App") processes, why we process it, and what choices you have. This policy applies to the Android game published under package name com.asrys.arrowgame.

Data We Process

The App is designed to collect the minimum data required to run gameplay statistics.

  • Device identifier: Android ID (used as a pseudonymous device identifier).
  • Gameplay data: puzzle seed, completion time, and play timestamp.
  • Aggregate stats: number of puzzles completed and total play time per device identifier.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or payment information.

Why We Process Data

  • To save game-related statistics tied to a device identifier.
  • To evaluate puzzle difficulty and improve game balance.
  • To monitor service health and prevent abuse.

Advertising and Tracking

Arrow Game does not serve third-party ads and does not use the Google Advertising ID for advertising or profiling.

Legal Basis (EEA/UK)

Where applicable, our processing is based on legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving the App.

Data Storage and Hosting

Gameplay data is sent to our backend API and stored in a managed PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway infrastructure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share your gameplay data with third parties for marketing. We may share limited data only with service providers required to operate the backend (for example, cloud hosting/database providers), or when required by law.

Data Retention

We keep gameplay logs and aggregate device statistics for as long as reasonably needed to operate and improve the App. We may delete or anonymize older data at our discretion.

Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. However, no internet transmission or storage system is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will review and delete it if appropriate.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. To submit a request, contact us using the email below and include enough detail for us to identify your records.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, depending on where our hosting providers operate.
