Privacy Policy for Arrow Game

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Arrow Game collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

  • Anonymous device identifier (for gameplay statistics only).
  • Gameplay data such as puzzle seed, completion time, and play timestamps.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

How We Use Information

  • To save and improve gameplay statistics.
  • To analyze puzzle performance and game balance.
  • To maintain service reliability and prevent abuse.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your gameplay data with third parties, except when required by law.

Data Retention

Gameplay and device-level statistics may be retained as long as needed to operate and improve the game.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect stored data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Arrow Game is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us to request help.

Changes to This Policy
